Nippon India Silver ETF FoF Direct Growth Nippon India Silver ETF FoF Direct Growth is a Commodities Mutual Fund Scheme launched by Nippon India Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 30 Jun 1995. Jitendra Tolani is the Current Fund Manager of Nippon India Silver ETF FoF Direct Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹7,48,581 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 07 Jul 2026 is ₹34.47.
The Nippon India Silver ETF FoF Direct Growth is rated Very High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹100. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹100. Exit load of 1% if redeemed within 15 days.
; Investment Objective The scheme seeks to provide returns that closely correspond to returns provided by Nippon India Silver ETF by investing in units of Nippon India Silver ETF.
